Fried Rice

  • Time : 20 minutes
  • Serves : 4 people

Ingredients

  1. Cooked Rice
  2. Oil
  3. Egg
  4. Garlic, and carrots
  5. Soy Sauce
  6. Mixed Vegetables
  7. Sausages
  8. Pork Mushroom Ball

    9. Step 1:Fry the rice by lightly browning in a pan or wok..
    Step 2:Stir-fry the onions, garlic, and carrots until tender.
    Step 3: Make a large well in the center of the pan with the rice.
    Step 4:Pour in the whisked eggs,sausages,pork mushroom ball and scramble until small curds form.
    Step 5:Soy sauce is optional but adds a savory flavor to the rice.
