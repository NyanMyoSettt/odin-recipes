French Fries

Ingredients

  1. Potatoes
  2. Water
  3. Oil
  4. Salt

Step 1:Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick. ...
Step 2:Soak them cold in water for at least an hour or overnight.
Step 3: Rinse them twice with cold water and pat the completely dry.
Step 4:Heat oil to 300 degrees. ...
Step 5:Increase heat to 400 degrees.
Step 6:Place them bake on paper towels and sprinkle immediately with salt.
