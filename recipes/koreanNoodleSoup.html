Korean Noodle Soup

  • Time : 10 minutes
  • Serves : 2 people

Ingredients

  1. Nongshim Shin Ramyun Black(1 pack)
  2. Water
  3. Egg
  4. Vegetables
  5. Sausages

Step 1:First,boil water 500ml in a pot.
Step 2:After 2mins,add Pork Mushroom Ball,Sausages.
Step 3: Add noodles, soup bases and vegetable mix.Cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Step 4:Add the egg.
